## Purpose
pfSense is our router and firewall platform.

## Hardware
Hardware is:
- Intel i3, dual core
- 2GB RAM
- 512 GB SSD
- Dual Nic motherboard

## Software
[pfSense Community Edition](https://pfsense.org)

## Configuration
pfSense is used primarily for routing and firewall.  The [[pihole]] provided DHCP and DNS services.
